About Gorewell

Built in the UK. Trusted worldwide.

Gorewell Ltd was founded to do distribution properly: connect quality suppliers with the markets that need them, move goods efficiently, and treat every relationship as a long-term one.

Our Story

Distribution without the friction

Global trade is full of middlemen who add cost without adding value. Gorewell exists to be the opposite: a single, accountable partner that handles sourcing, procurement and delivery end-to-end, so our customers get the right products, at the right price, exactly when they need them.

From our UK head office we buy and sell across five continents — serving more than 60 countries through a network of over 1,000 trusted suppliers. Whether it's a single sourcing brief or an ongoing wholesale programme, we run every order with the same discipline: clear communication, honest pricing and dependable dispatch.

UK Based. Globally Connected.
Our Values

What we stand for

Four principles guide every deal we do — from a first sample order to a multi-year supply partnership.

Integrity

We do what we say. Honest pricing, straight answers and no surprises — even when the news is difficult.

Reliability

On time, every time. Agreed dispatch standards, tracked shipments and replenishment you can plan around.

Partnership

We optimise for the long term. The best deals are the ones both sides want to repeat, year after year.

Efficiency

Lean by design. Fewer hands on every shipment means lower cost, faster movement and less that can go wrong.

How We Operate

One accountable process, four steps

Every engagement — from a one-off sourcing brief to a standing wholesale programme — runs through the same disciplined process.

Listen & Source

We start with your requirements — product, volume, target price, timeline — then source options from our vetted global supplier network.

Verify & Procure

Samples, specifications and compliance are checked before we commit. You approve exactly what will ship, at a locked-in price.

Move & Deliver

We manage freight, customs and documentation end-to-end, with agreed dispatch timelines and visibility at every stage.

Support & Grow

After delivery we review performance together and set up replenishment cycles, so reordering gets easier every time.
